Job: Recruiting Coordinator

About This Job: LinkedIn was built to help professionals achieve more in their careers, and every day millions of people use our products to make connections, discover opportunities and gain insights. Our global reach means we get to make a direct impact on the world’s workforce in ways no other company can. We’re much more than a digital resume – we transform lives through innovative products and technology.

We embrace diversity, inclusion and belonging as a core value of our culture and operating rhythm across Talent Acquisition globally. Diversity of all forms - abilities, age, ethnic backgrounds, races, career experiences, and more – is what propels us closer towards realizing LinkedIn’s vision of creating economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce. We are looking for a Recruiting Coordinator who is passionate about providing a best-on-planet experience for our candidates, excellent at building strong relationships with clients, and loves multi-tasking and problem-solving in a fast-paced environment. If this sounds like you, come join us!

You will be responsible for partnering closely with recruiters and various hiring teams to coordinate and schedule phone, video, and onsite interviews for candidates. You will need to pay excellent attention to details, have superior organizational and time management skills, the ability to communicate concisely and effectively, while juggling multiple tasks and meeting strict deadlines. We are looking for someone who is a team player, shows initiative, and is able to navigate complex scenarios with a sense of urgency.

Responsibilities: Coordinate several phases of the recruiting process, including scheduling phone & video interviews, onsite interviews, batch days, and organizing travel arrangements, if necessary Execute scheduling requests accurately within deadlines Provide updates to candidates, recruiters, and hiring teams regarding interview status Greet candidates onsite and ensure they have an exceptional and welcoming experience at LinkedIn Develop strong internal client and stakeholder relationships and maintain effective communication channels Multi-task, problem-solve, and effectively prioritize in a fast-paced, dynamic environment Maintain the data integrity of your candidates in the applicant tracking system ( Jobvite ) and digital workflow tracker (ServiceNow) Contribute to various recruiting projects & duties, on an as needed basis

Minimum Qualifications: Bachelor’s degree Experience with Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook

Preferred Qualifications: 1+ years of Recruiting Coordination experience 1+ years of experience using an ATS ; Jobvite or SmartRecruiters are a plus! 1+ years of experience using a digital workflow tracker, such as ServiceNow Rigorous attention to detail and excellent organizational skills Solution-oriented, reliable, and able to anticipate both candidate and hiring team needs Ability to stay calm in high pressure situations and fast-paced environments Strong written and verbal communication skills Willingness to recommend and drive process improvements and innovation Familiarity with scheduling in multiple time zones Passionate about candidate experience, as well as LinkedIn’s products, mission, vision, and culture
